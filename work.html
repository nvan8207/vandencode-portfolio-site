Mathematics — Wolfram Mathematica
3D Geometry & Linear Systems
University mathematics project (MTH1030) combining 3D vector geometry and linear algebra in Mathematica. Reconstructed a partially-submerged cube's coordinates using cross products, line equations, and box products — then computed edge lengths, surface area, and volume above the ground plane with a full 3D visualisation. Also solved a 5×5 matter-transporter system of linear equations, analysed consistency and uniqueness, and derived error-correction vectors.
